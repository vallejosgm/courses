About Us!

Gean's Pizza

The Gean's Pizza team was committed to upholding a tradition of original pizza flavor. Maintaining more than 50 years making pizza manually, without letting the machines intervene so as not to remove the traditional flavor from each processed dough. Our mission is to make pizza a delight, it is to make the pizza that you imagine for your day or special event. And remember that nothing is frozen here. We are from the old school!
