At Gean's Pizza, the machines are left out! The Gean's Pizza is going ahead! Our dough is made from scratch by hand, cared for and tested twice for 24 hours, rolled and mixed in the traditional way, without machines for an unmatched flavor. The Toppings, every morning, are processed, crushed and poured over the pizza. If you have something in mind, place your order, for us it's not a job, it's a challenge. There is nothing frozen here. We are old school!
